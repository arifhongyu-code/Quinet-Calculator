Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Welcome to Quinet Calculator ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit www.quinetcalc.com (the "Site"). By using our Site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Enter Into Calculators

All calculations on Quinet Calculator are performed entirely in your browser. Numbers you type into our calculators (such as salary, age, loan amount) are never sent to our servers and are not stored, logged, or shared.

b) Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our Site, we and our third-party partners may automatically collect:

  • Your IP address and approximate location (country/city level)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website (how you found us)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

This data is collected through cookies and similar tracking technologies and is used for analytics and advertising purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Site
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • To analyze traffic patterns and understand how visitors use our calculators
  • To detect and prevent technical issues or abuse

3. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

Type Purpose
Essential Basic site functionality
Analytics Google Analytics — understand site usage
Advertising Google AdSense — display personalised ads

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the Site.

4. Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this Site. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or www.aboutads.info.

Google's use of advertising cookies is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our Site. Google Analytics collects data such as pages visited, time on site, and general location. This data is aggregated and anonymised. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Quinet Calculator is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights (GDPR / UK GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to object — object to processing for direct marketing
  • Right to restrict processing — request we limit how we use your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address in the Contact section below.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion of your personal information, and to opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell personal information. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

10. Data Security

Since all calculator inputs are processed locally in your browser and never transmitted to our servers, the risk of data exposure from calculator use is minimal. We implement reasonable security measures to protect any data we do collect.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
